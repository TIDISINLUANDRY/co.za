About Us

Nokuphila Bele, the visionary founder behind Tidis in Laundry, has established a thriving enterprise situated in the vibrant city of Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. With a deep-rooted belief in the transformative power of entrepreneurship, Nokuphila champions the ethos that businesses like Tidis not only drive economic growth but also play a pivotal role in alleviating unemployment.

Moreover, Nokuphila's commitment to social responsibility extends beyond job creation. Tidis in Laundry actively engages with local initiatives aimed at empowering women and youth, further contributing to the socio-economic development of the community.
